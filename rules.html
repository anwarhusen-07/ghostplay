RULES OF THE GHOST WORLD

  • You have 5 hearts ❤️ (lives).
  • Lose 1 heart for each wrong answer.
  • Three levels: Simple, Medium, Hard.
  • Answer 7 questions correctly to unlock the next level.
  • Perfect score on first attempt earns a GHOSTPLAY badge.
  • Switching windows will terminate your quiz and return you to the rules page.
  • Beware the midnight curse: Wrong answers summon spirits!
  • Survive all levels to become a Ghostplay Grandmaster!
